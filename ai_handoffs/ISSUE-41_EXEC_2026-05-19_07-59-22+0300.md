### Tests

- `crates/cad-projection/tests/face_picking_smoke.rs::pick_resolves_loft_top_cap_face`:
  new Test 11. Builds a direct `OperatorNode::Loft` root from a CCW
  unit-square bottom profile (`[0,0],[1,0],[1,1],[0,1]`) to a CCW
  larger-square top profile (`[0,0],[3,0],[3,3],[0,3]`) with `length = 1.0`,
  commits it, spawns/projects it through `CadProjection`, and sets
  `BRepHandle.brep_owner = Some(ENTITY_OWNER)` post-spawn before the
  projection tick. Casts a ray straight down (`origin [2.0, 1.0, 5.0]`,
  `direction [0,0,-1]`) through the top-cap interior and calls
  `CadProjection::pick_face`. Asserts all three identity fields:
  `pick.entity` == the projected Loft-root entity, `pick.owner` ==
  `ENTITY_OWNER`, and `pick.face_id` ==
  `BRepFaceId::for_loft_face(ENTITY_OWNER, LoftFaceTag::Top)`. Also asserts
  `pick.t > 0.0` and `pick.t ≈ 4.0` (top cap at `z=+1.0`, ray origin
  `z=+5`).

  Ray-target reasoning: the top cap is `profile_b` (`[0,3]×[0,3]`) lifted
  to `z=+1.0`, fan-triangulated from vertex `[0,0]` across the diagonal
  `y = x`. `[2.0, 1.0]` is interior to the larger square and strictly
  inside the lower-right fan triangle (`[0,0],[3,0],[3,3]`) since
  `1.0 < 2.0` — no cap edge/vertex or fan-diagonal ambiguity. It is outside
  the `[0,1]×[0,1]` bottom cap, so the Top cap is the only resolvable cap
  hit along the ray.
