## What I Changed

### Source

- `crates/editor-shell/src/camera.rs`: added `EditorCameraState::pan_in_view_plane`, which translates `eye` and `target` by the same finite view-plane vector. It rejects zero deltas, non-finite cursor deltas, invalid viewport size, non-finite/degenerate eye-target vectors, non-finite/degenerate up vectors, invalid FOV scale, and non-finite results before mutating camera state.
- `crates/editor-shell/src/lifecycle/viewport_navigation.rs`: added `ViewportPanDrag`, mirroring the existing orbit drag shape. It starts only from finite cursor positions, stops by clearing the stored cursor, and advances its cursor anchor only when camera pan math reports an actual pan.
- `crates/editor-shell/src/lifecycle/mod.rs`: added `viewport_pan_drag` to `EditorShell`, initialized it in all constructors, updated active pan drags on `CursorMoved`, started pan on middle-button press over the Viewport tab body, and stopped pan unconditionally on middle-button release.

### Tests

- `crates/editor-shell/src/lifecycle/tests.rs::viewport_middle_button_pan_starts_only_with_finite_cursor_and_viewport_hit`: covers missing cursor, no-host hit-test false, non-viewport start, non-finite start cursor, and valid gated start behavior.
- `crates/editor-shell/src/lifecycle/tests.rs::viewport_middle_button_pan_cursor_delta_translates_eye_and_target_in_view_plane`: verifies concrete cursor-delta pan math and preservation of eye-target offset, up, FOV, near, and far.
- `crates/editor-shell/src/lifecycle/tests.rs::viewport_middle_button_pan_release_stops_future_cursor_translation`: verifies release/stop prevents later cursor movement from translating the camera.
- `crates/editor-shell/src/lifecycle/tests.rs::viewport_middle_button_pan_no_active_zero_delta_and_non_finite_cursor_are_no_ops`: covers inactive drag, zero delta, and non-finite cursor update no-ops.
- `crates/editor-shell/src/lifecycle/tests.rs::viewport_middle_button_pan_degenerate_or_non_finite_camera_vectors_are_no_ops`: covers degenerate eye-target, degenerate up, and non-finite eye vector no-ops.
