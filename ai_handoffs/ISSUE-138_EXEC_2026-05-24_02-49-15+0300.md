  editor-shell audit ledger.

  pins that Ctrl+Z after Ctrl+4 restores `time_scale().value()` to
  `TimeScale::DEFAULT`.
- `crates/editor-shell/tests/time_scale_test.rs::repeated_ctrl_4_at_max_is_noop`:
  pins that a second Ctrl+4 while already at `TimeScale::MAX` does not push
  a stack entry, does not advance the cursor, does not change the dirty
  flag, and does not append a second `TimeScaleChanged` audit event â€” i.e.
  the existing `set_time_scale` no-op short-circuit remains authoritative
  for repeat Ctrl+4.
