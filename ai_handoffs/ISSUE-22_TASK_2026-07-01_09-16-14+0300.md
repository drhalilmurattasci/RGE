## Acceptance Criteria

- The effective-bindings list is deterministic and ordered exactly as
  `ResolveResult::bindings()` yields it; no raw `HashMap` order or ad hoc
  unstable sorting is introduced in `editor-egui-host`.
- The effective-bindings rows display executable shortcut-to-command bindings
  only. Passive Play hints such as Space/Escape remain visible in the existing
  menu-order help view but are not treated as executable effective bindings.
- Existing menu-order shortcut-help rows keep their File/Edit/Play/View/Plugins
  group order and entry order.
- Disabled menu rows remain present and disabled in the menu-order view.
- Conflict annotations continue to come from the resolved/projected conflict
  diagnostics and include ordered peer entry ids.
- The shortcut-help projection and window do not enqueue, dispatch, or route
  any `Command`; command activation remains owned by existing menu and command
  palette paths.
- The default resolved menu remains exactly 19 executable accelerators with
  zero conflicts.
- No prohibited surface changes; `editor-ui` is read-only for this dispatch.
