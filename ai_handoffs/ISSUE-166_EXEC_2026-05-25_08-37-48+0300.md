## Open Questions for Reviewer

- First draft of the integration tests fetched `world.entity(id).unwrap().get::<T>()` in a single chained expression. The `EntityRef` returned by `World::entity` is a temporary; chaining `.get::<T>()` through it triggers `error[E0716]: temporary value dropped while borrowed`.
