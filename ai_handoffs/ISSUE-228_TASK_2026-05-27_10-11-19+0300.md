    normal queue log is missing or incomplete;

  - comment on the GitHub issue with the branch, commit, verification/control

    `ai-dispatch-failed`, retry labels, and stale taxonomy labels according to
    the outcome;
  - keep `ai-dispatch-done` only for positive salvage;
  - close as completed only after the useful work is merged or explicitly
    accepted, otherwise leave the issue open or close it as not planned with a
    reason.

  `[DONE-SUPERSEDED ...]` tasks are intentionally consumed or superseded by a

  should preserve original text for provenance.

  labels and describes their purpose:

  - `ai-dispatch-failure-timeout`
