- The test constructs an `AnimGraph` with a diamond and a downstream path, for example `root -> left`, `root -> right`, `left -> join`, `right -> join`, and `join -> tail`.
- The test uses `rge_kernel_graph_foundation::Invalidation` and an `InvalidationListener` recorder to capture delivered dirty nodes.
