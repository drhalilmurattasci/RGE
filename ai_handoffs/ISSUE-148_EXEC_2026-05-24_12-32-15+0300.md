## Halt Diagnosis

The TASK packet's first listed halt condition is:

> `golden-projects/simple-scene/.rge-project` does not parse as
> `rge_data::Project` without production-code or golden-project changes.

That condition is triggered. Two schema-vs-manifest divergences make a
positive schema-load assertion impossible without editing either
`crates/rge-data/src/**` (forbidden) or
`golden-projects/simple-scene/.rge-project` (forbidden):

1. **`TargetTier` wire form.** `crates/rge-data/src/project.rs` rows
   63-74 declare
   `#[serde(rename_all = "lowercase")] pub enum TargetTier { Desktop, Mobile, Web, Headless }`.
   RON deserializes a `Vec<TargetTier>` of that shape from bare
   identifiers — the canonical on-disk form, reproduced verbatim in
   `crates/rge-data/tests/fixtures/sample_project.rge-project` rows 5-8,
   is `target_tiers: [\n    desktop,\n    headless,\n],`. The golden
   manifest at `golden-projects/simple-scene/.rge-project` row 5 instead
   writes `target_tiers: ["desktop"],` — a *quoted* string. RON's parser
   rejects that with `5:20: Expected identifier` (the `"` at column 20
   is the failure site), and the positive
   `ron::from_str::<Project>(...)` call never reaches the assertion
   block. The same divergence appears in all six golden manifests
   (`simple-scene`, `material-zoo`, `skinned-character`,
   `physics-puzzle`, `cad-parametric`, and `stress-world` all carry
   `target_tiers: ["desktop"]` on row 5), so this is a
   placeholder-vs-schema policy issue across the entire
   `golden-projects/` tree, not a simple-scene-specific typo.

2. **Unknown `schema_version` field.** Row 8 of every golden manifest
   declares `schema_version: "0.1.0"`, but `crates/rge-data/src/project
   .rs` rows 27-44 declare no such field on `Project`. Even after the
   `target_tiers` issue above is hypothetically resolved, this second
   divergence is a candidate for an additional parse failure. It was
   flagged but not separately reduced because the prior step already
   blocks parsing.

The previous dispatch's audit
(`ai_handoffs/ISSUE-146_EXEC_2026-05-24_11-42-07+0300.md`) flagged the
`schema_version` divergence under Q4/D3 as a policy decision pending
human review. The `target_tiers` quoting divergence is a related but
independent finding surfaced for the first time by attempting the
schema-load test the present TASK packet describes.

Because both divergences live in `golden-projects/**` and
`crates/rge-data/src/**` respectively, and the TASK packet's MUST NOT
list forbids edits to either surface, the TASK's deliverable cannot be
implemented without a fresh Planner packet that scopes the
manifest-vs-schema reconciliation.
