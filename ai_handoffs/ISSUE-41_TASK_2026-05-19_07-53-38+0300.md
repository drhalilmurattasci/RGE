## Deliverables

- Exactly one new Loft-focused face-picking smoke test in
  crates/cad-projection/tests/face_picking_smoke.rs.
- The test builds a direct `OperatorNode::Loft` root from a square profile to a
  larger square profile, commits it, spawns/projects it through
  `CadProjection`, and sets `BRepHandle.brep_owner` to a known
  `BRepOwnerId`.
- The test casts a ray through the Loft top cap and calls
  `CadProjection::pick_face`.
- The test asserts the returned `FacePick` has the expected entity, owner, and
  `BRepFaceId::for_loft_face(owner, LoftFaceTag::Top)`.
- The execution report documents the exact source change and verification
  result.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The new test is integration-test-only and does not rely on production code
